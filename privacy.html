Privacy Policy

Scope of this Notice

Protecting your personal data is a top priority at BCH Consulting. This privacy policy describes how we collect, process, and protect your data in connection with our online presence and services in vehicle sales, maintenance, repairs, and customer support.

Application of this Notice

This notice applies to personal data processed by BCH Consulting as the data controller under applicable data protection laws. In some cases, we may also act as a data processor. "Personal data" includes any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

Online Tracking and Google Ads

BCH Consulting uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance the functionality of our website and provide you with an optimal user experience. We also use Google Ads for advertising purposes. This involves collecting information about your interactions with our ads and website, including clicks, pages visited, and conversion activities. Google uses cookies to collect and process this data. This information helps us optimize our advertising campaigns and display more relevant ads. You can adjust your preferences for personalized advertising in your Google account or restrict cookie usage in your browser.

Data Security

BCH Consulting implements state-of-the-art technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. This includes strict confidentiality agreements, role-based access controls, and regular security training for our team.

Your Data Protection Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to information
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability

Data Retention

BCH Consulting retains your data only for as long as necessary for the purposes stated or as required by law. The retention period depends on the type of data and the purpose of its collection.

Protection of Minors' Privacy

Our digital platforms are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors unless necessary for specific services.

Updates to this Notice

BCH Consulting reserves the right to update this privacy policy as needed. Significant changes will be communicated on our digital platforms.

Contact

For questions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us at info@bchconsult.co.za.
